What We Are
Built on three foundations.

Encounter

Every session is designed as an honest meeting with the person of Jesus — not a lecture, but a living encounter with scripture that demands a response.

Community

Teenagers, young adults, and parents sit together around one table. Every voice matters. No one is too young to ask the hard questions.

Truth

We go where the evidence leads — honestly, openly, and without pretence. Faith that cannot withstand a question is not yet the faith that sets people free.

How The Table works.

Prep Guide Released

Two weeks before each session, the Student Prep Guide goes live. Read the passages, journal the questions, come ready.

2

The WhatsApp link goes to the Student Guide. Every family, teenager, and young adult can access it instantly.

First Sunday of the month, two hours together. Story, structure, and open reflection — in that order.
